ponyo's ramen with ham

ingredients

  • 1 egg, soft-boiled (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup broth (chicken or beef)
  • 1 packet instant ramen without seasoning packet
  • 1 baby bok choy, cut to bite-sized pieces
  • 3 slices ham
  • 1 tbsp green onion, chopped
  • chili oil (optional)

steps

for soft-boiled egg:

  1. bring a small pot of water to boil.
  2. turn to medium-low heat and slowly lower the egg into the pan.
  3. simmer for 5-6 minutes.

for ramen:

  1. add the butter to a small saucepan and heat over medium-low heat until melted.
  2. add garlic and curry paste. stir to release fragrance, for about a minute.
  3. add coconut milk and bring to a boil over medium heat.
  4. add the broth and let cook until boiling again.
  5. add the instant ramen, baby bok choy, and ham.
  6. simmer until ramen is cooked, around 3 minutes, then serve.
